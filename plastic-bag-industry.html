Wicketer Bag Machine Belts are our Speciality. Superior Band's recent acquisition of Lambeth Band Corporation has positioned us as the global leader in wicketer belts.

Learn More

In the late 1980's, Superior Bands Inc. teamed up with a smaller bag Machine company named Roan. We developed elastic rope cotton belts belts for their new wicketer machines. They also reached out to us to develop a flat sewn cotton endless belt for their take away conveyors in 1" and 4 7/8" wide lengths.
