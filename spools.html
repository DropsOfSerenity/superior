Spools

We stock both Regular Spools and Split Spools for your Line Shaft Conveyors...Our Regular Spools are tough and manufactured to keep up with the daily operations of a large Distribution Center.

Our Split spools will keep things moving and lower your down time. No more disassembling your conveyor to replace spools! These split spools will save you time and money. This is a 2-piece spool that snaps together for easy installation. They will not fall off or come open once installed. There are two types available. The red spool is standard size and the green spool is speed up size which dramatically increases roller speed.
